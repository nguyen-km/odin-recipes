Palak Paneer

Description

An Indian palak paneer recipe.

Ingredients

  • 24 oz frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 cup yellow onion, chopped
  • 1.5 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1.5 tsp tumeric
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 serrano peppers, chopped
  • 5 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1.5 tsp tumeric

Steps

  1. Toss paneer cubes in tumeric, cayenne, salt, and oil
  2. Heat up a large skillet over medium and fry panner cubes. Remove paneer from skillet and set aside.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low. Add oil, onion, garlic, ginger, and Serrano chile to skillet. Cook until browned.
  4. Add garam masala, and cumin to skillet. Add a touch to water to prevent spices from burning.
  5. Raise heat to medium. Add spinach and 1/2 cup of water to skillet. Mix well and cook uncovered for 5-8 minutes or until most of the water has been absorbed (15 mins if cooking from frozen)
  6. Add heavy cream to skillet and let cook until well incorporated, stirring occasionally.
  7. Mix in panner and serve.
